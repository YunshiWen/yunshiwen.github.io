## Interpretable Reinforcement Learning

Using Neuro-Symbolic models (e.g., Logic Neural Network) to learn interpretable policies for reinforcement learning problems. We use neuro-symbolic models to replace the conventional fully-connected neural networks in policy gradient algorithms. Experiment results shows that our method can learn interpretable policies in the form of logic formulas while maintaining comparable performance as the state-of-the-art RL algorithms.
